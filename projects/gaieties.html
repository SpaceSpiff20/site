Gaieties 2025

Director Stanford, CA September 2025 – November 2025

Overview:
Gaieties,a longstanding tradition at Stanford University, is an annual, student-written musical extravaganza dating back to 1911. Performed the week before Big Game against Cal, Gaieties is a culmination of the unpredictable, the wonderful, the mundane, the infuriating, and everything in between, all wrapped up in our shared desire to beat Berkeley.

Responsibilities:
Being the director, I was responsible for helping hold auditions, casting the show, rehearsing the cast, and overseeing the production of the show. Auditions started the first week of school and rehearsals began the following week. In order to ensure we had enough rehearsal time, we would rehearse up to a minimum of 20 hours a week.
