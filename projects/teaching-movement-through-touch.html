About this Project

Lead Researcher Sept 2025 – Present

Teaching Movement Through Touch: An EMG-Driven Haptic Feedback System

This research project explores the use of Electromyography (EMG) signals combined with haptic feedback to create a closed-loop system for motor learning. We are investigating how direct tactile feedback can accelerate the acquisition of complex movements and improve proprioception.

The page will feature detailed methodologies, system diagrams, and preliminary results from our user studies.
