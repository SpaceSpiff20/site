SkinCall

Cross-disciplinary Researcher and Developer Los Angeles, CA | June 2024 – July 2024

Overview:
At SkinCall, I worked at the intersection of medicine and technology, developing tools to assist in dermatology.

Key Achievements:

  • Created machine-learning ready classification algorithms aimed at assisting dermatological diagnosis.
  • Acted as a technical liaison, bridging the gap between practicing dermatologists and computer vision specialists to ensure tool relevance and accuracy.

Technologies Used:
Python Machine Learning Computer Vision

Expanded Details

The primary work done at SkinCall was to answer the question of "can we create an image classification system that uses kernel filters based on specific pathological diagnosing". My primary job was working with dermatologists to understand differential diagnosis processes. What do they look for visually, what questions do they ask patients, etc. I then worked with computer vision specialists to create kernels designed to replicate this process.
